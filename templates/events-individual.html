Chess Championship

Overview

Ladies and gentlemen Aitchison College Renaissance Cup offers you a chance to demonstrate your strategic and intellectual skills in the finest board game know to man (after ludo of course).Tired of playing online with random strangers? Not to worry Aitchison College finally gives chess lovers all over Pakistan an oppurtunity to play against students from all over the country in the first installment of ARC.We hope to see on the 16th of October and expect to see a great deal of competition and display of skills. May the odds ever be in you favor.

Good Luck and be sure to keep your king safe!

Spellathon

Overview

Looking forward to seeing you all at one of Aitchison Renaissance Cup’s most anticipated event. As evident from the name, we aim to test student’s vocabulary at this event. Through a combination of a number of different interesting rounds we will ensure that this event is competitive and interesting at the same time. Another ironic aspect of this event is the fact that you don’t require any major preparation; instead the most avid reader will most likely emerge victorious at Spellathon.

Good Luck!
