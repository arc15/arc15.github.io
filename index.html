Aitchison Renaissance Cup

Presented by the Aitchison College Vitruvian Society

A brainchild of the Aitchison College Vitruvian Society. The Aitchison Renaissance Cup is an event that puts to test alternative talents and abilities that do not get showcased in conventional competitive events. With the first edition we aim to exhibit three abilities with more to follow in succesive editions.

We have partnered with multiple institutions whose specialities range from Chess to Writing in organising the event.

Put your grit to the test, awaiting you this October.
